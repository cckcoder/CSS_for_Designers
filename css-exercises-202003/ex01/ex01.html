Skooldio

With the accelerating pace of technological change, education can no longer end in college. At Skooldio, we believe in lifelong learning. Through online, in-person, and blended learning, we strive to help individuals master today’s most in-demand skills — programming, data science, design thinking, digital business — and empower businesses to build the workforce of the future and stay at the forefront of their industry.

Founded by ex-Facebook data scientist, Skooldio seeks only the best industry experts from world-leading companies to teach and engage with our clients. Our courses and workshops are specifically designed to encourage learning by doing through exercises, projects, and group activities. As a technology company, we continuously develop education technologies that can effectively enhance teaching and learning experience.
